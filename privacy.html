Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information.

Effective Date: April 18, 2026 · Last Updated: April 18, 2026

NJ Developments LLC (“NJ Developments,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. It applies to our website at nj-developments.com, our SMS messaging program, and any other services we provide (collectively, the “Services”).

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you give us directly, information we receive automatically, and information from third-party services.

Information you provide

  • Name, email address, phone number, and business name when you fill out our contact form, book a consultation, or reply to an outreach message.
  • Project details you share during calls, messages, or discovery sessions.
  • SMS opt-in consent when you agree to receive text messages from us.

Information collected automatically

  • IP address, browser type, device type, operating system, referring URL, and pages visited.
  • Analytics data via Google Analytics (GA4), including page views, session duration, and event interactions.

Information from third parties

  • Calendar and scheduling data when you book a call through Calendly.
  • Publicly listed business contact information (for B2B outreach only).

2. SMS Messaging and Toll-Free Program

If you provide your phone number and opt in to text messages from NJ Developments, we may send you SMS messages from our toll-free number +1 (855) 640-6166.

NJ Developments will not share, sell, rent, transfer, or otherwise disclose your SMS opt-in data or consent to any third party for marketing, promotional, or any other purpose. Your consent applies only to NJ Developments.

Message types you may receive

  • Responses to inquiries you send us.
  • Appointment confirmations and reminders.
  • Service updates, project status, and follow-up questions.
  • Occasional outreach to business owners we have identified publicly.

Frequency, cost, and opt-out

  • Message frequency varies. You will not receive more messages than are needed to handle your inquiry or project.
  • Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier and plan.
  • To stop messages, reply STOP at any time. You will receive a single confirmation message and then no further messages.
  • For help, reply HELP or email us at the address below.
  • Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your inquiries and deliver the services you request.
  • To schedule and conduct consultations, meetings, and project work.
  • To send SMS and email communications you have consented to receive.
  • To improve our website, services, and customer experience.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only in these limited cases:

  • Service providers who help us operate (for example, Google Analytics for analytics, Twilio for SMS delivery, Calendly for scheduling, Firebase for hosting). These providers are contractually limited to using your information only to provide their services to us.
  • Legal compliance when required by law, subpoena, or to protect rights, safety, or property.
  • Business transfers in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets — with advance notice when legally required.

SMS opt-in data and consent are never shared with any third party, under any circumstance, for any reason.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services, each with its own privacy practices:

  • Google Analytics (GA4) — website analytics.
  • Twilio — SMS delivery.
  • Calendly — appointment scheduling.
  • Firebase — website hosting.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your relationship with us is active or as needed to provide services, meet legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. SMS opt-in records are retained for the duration of your subscription and a reasonable period afterward to document consent, as required by carrier and regulatory rules.

7. Your Rights and Choices

  • Opt out of SMS by replying STOP.
  • Opt out of emails by using the unsubscribe link in any marketing email or by contacting us directly.
  • Access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you by contacting us.
  • Do Not Track: our site does not respond to DNT signals, but you can control analytics cookies through your browser settings.

Depending on your state (for example, California, Colorado, Connecticut, Virginia), you may have additional rights under applicable privacy law. Contact us to exercise any of these rights.

8. Security

We use industry-standard safeguards (TLS encryption, access controls, vetted third-party providers) to protect your information. No system is perfectly secure, but we take reasonable steps to keep your data safe and will notify you if a breach affects your information.

9. Children’s Privacy

Our Services are intended for business owners and are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with information, contact us and we will delete it.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the “Last Updated” date at the top of this page. Material changes will be communicated through the website or by direct notice where appropriate.
