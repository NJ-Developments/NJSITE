FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the questions we hear most from contractors.

What does NJ Developments do for contractors?

We build professional websites, set up AI voice agents that answer your calls 24/7, automate Google review requests after every job, and handle your SEO so homeowners find you on Google. Everything a contractor needs to get more jobs without spending time on marketing.

How fast can you build my website?

Most contractor websites go from concept to launch in 1-2 weeks. We move fast because we know you need to start getting leads, not wait around for months.

I already get work from referrals. Why do I need a website?

Referrals are great, but they have a ceiling. A website means homeowners searching for your trade in your area find you too. That's a second pipeline of leads you're currently missing. And when someone gets a referral, the first thing they do is Google you. If you don't show up, you lose the job before you know about it.

What's an AI voice agent?

It's an AI that answers your phone when you can't. If you're on a job site, driving, or it's after hours, the voice agent picks up, gets the caller's name, what they need, and their contact info, then texts it all to you. You call them back when you're free instead of losing them to the next contractor who picks up.

Do you provide ongoing support?

Yes. We handle hosting, updates, and support on a monthly basis. You focus on running your business, we keep everything running online.
